About Visit Mongolia Tours

Our Story

Visit Mongolia Tours was created from real travel and tourism experience.

The founder of this website has traveled to more than 20 countries and over 50 cities around the world, both independently and through tour companies.

Over the last 10 years, the founder has worked in Mongolia’s tourism industry as a tour guide and cook, serving international travelers from many different countries.

Through these experiences, the idea for this website was born — to help travelers find simple, useful, and trustworthy information about Mongolia travel, tours, destinations, and local experiences.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make traveling in Mongolia easier and more comfortable by providing clear travel information, recommended routes, destination ideas, and useful booking options.

We want more travelers to discover Mongolia’s beautiful nature, nomadic culture, and unique experiences.
