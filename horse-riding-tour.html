Tour Overview

This horse riding tour is designed for travelers who want to discover Mongolia’s raw beauty, mountain landscapes and peaceful lakes while enjoying a real outdoor adventure.

About the Tour

During this trip you will travel through beautiful mountain valleys and highland scenery, ride local horses, camp in the wilderness and enjoy traditional Mongolian meals. One of the highlights of the journey is reaching a landscape often described as the “Yosemite of Mongolia.”

Day 1: Drive from Ulaanbaatar and begin horse riding to the campsite.
Day 2: Full rest day, hiking, photography and lake exploration.
Day 3: Main horse riding day through mountain and lake scenery.
Day 4: Final ride, return to vehicle and drive back to Ulaanbaatar.

Detailed Itinerary

Comfortable pace, rest time and scenic stops are included throughout the journey.

Day 1

Ulaanbaatar – Starting Point – Horse Riding to Camp

Breakfast in the morning. The guides load the luggage and the group departs around 10:00 AM. After arrival at the starting point, horses and gear are prepared and the adventure begins.

  • Drive from Ulaanbaatar to the riding area
  • Safety briefing and horse preparation
  • Horse riding for about 3–4 hours
  • Approximate riding distance: 10–12 km
  • Arrival at a scenic area known as the “Yosemite of Mongolia”
  • Camp setup, dinner and campfire
Day 2

Rest Day – Lakes, Hiking and Nature Exploration

This day is dedicated to rest and exploration. The region features 7–8 connected lakes fed by mountain streams from the Yan Saridag range, creating a truly beautiful natural landscape.

  • No horse riding on this day
  • Morning breakfast at camp
  • Hike toward the Kherkhluuriin Saridag peak area
  • Free time for photography and walking by the lakes
  • Traditional hot pot meal in the evening
  • Relax around the campfire and overnight camping
Day 3

Main Horse Riding Day

After breakfast, the luggage is loaded again and the journey continues deeper through mountain and lake scenery. This is the main riding day of the tour.

  • Breakfast at camp
  • Horse riding for more than 5 hours
  • Approximate riding distance: 15–20 km
  • 2–3 rest stops along the route
  • Light snacks and packed food during the day
  • Evening camp, dinner and rest
Day 4

Final Ride – Return to Vehicle – Drive to Ulaanbaatar

The final morning includes breakfast, camp packing and a short final horse ride before reaching the vehicle and returning to Ulaanbaatar.

  • Breakfast and camp packing
  • Short final horse riding section
  • Return to vehicle around the late afternoon
  • Drive back to Ulaanbaatar
  • Estimated arrival in the city around 11:00 PM
