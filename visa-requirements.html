Required Documents

  • Passport valid for at least 6 months beyond intended travel.
  • Completed visa application form.
  • Passport-size photo, usually 3.5 × 4.5 cm.
  • Visa fee payment proof.
  • Travel plan, hotel booking, or invitation letter depending on visa type.
  • Proof of sufficient funds may be requested in some cases.

Main Visa Routes

  • Visa-free entry: available for some nationalities for a limited stay.
  • eVisa: online application for eligible nationalities and trip purposes.
  • Embassy / Consular visa: for travelers who are not visa-exempt or not eligible for eVisa.
  • Other categories: business, employment, student, official and transit visas may require extra permissions.

Visa Fees and Processing

Exact fees depend on nationality, visa type, mission, and processing route. Use the official system for final pricing.

Tourist Visa

Tourist visa fees are commonly presented by the official visa system or the relevant Mongolian diplomatic mission. Non-refundable fee rules may apply.

eVisa

The official eVisa portal handles application, payment, and visa delivery online. Payment rules and support contacts are shown inside the official system.

Tip: Do not rely on third-party blogs for final fee amounts. Always verify current pricing on the official eVisa portal or the official diplomatic mission website.

What to Prepare Before Applying

  • Your passport information page scan.
  • A clear passport photo that meets size requirements.
  • Your arrival and departure plan.
  • Accommodation booking or local host information when relevant.
  • Bank or payment card ready for official visa fees.

Important Travel Notes

  • Visa rules can change, especially visa-exempt arrangements.
  • Do not book non-refundable travel until you verify your visa status.
  • Keep printed copies of important documents when using embassy or consular channels.
  • For “Other / Not listed” countries, always use the official checker.
