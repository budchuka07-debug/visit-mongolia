Mongolia Destinations

Places to Visit in Mongolia

Explore Mongolia by region — from the Gobi Desert and ancient valleys to northern lakes, western mountains and short routes near Ulaanbaatar.

Central Mongolia

Arkhangai

Hot springs, mountains, volcanic scenery, ancient valleys and peaceful countryside routes.
Western Mongolia

Bayan-Ulgii

Altai mountains, eagle hunters, glaciers, Kazakh culture and dramatic western landscapes.
Eastern Mongolia

Khentii

Forest valleys, rivers, sacred history and the birthplace region of Chinggis Khan.
Northern Mongolia

Khuvsgul

The Blue Pearl lake, taiga forest, reindeer culture and relaxing lakeside journeys.
Northern Route

Selenge

Rivers, monasteries, fertile valleys and green landscapes near the northern border.
Near Ulaanbaatar

Tuv

Terelj, Hustai, monasteries, short private trips and beautiful steppe scenery.
Gobi Desert

Umnugovi

Khongor dunes, Bayanzag flaming cliffs, Yol Valley and iconic desert adventures.
History & Culture

Uvurkhangai

Kharkhorin, Erdene Zuu, Orkhon Valley, Tuvkhun monastery and heritage routes.
