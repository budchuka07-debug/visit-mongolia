Detailed Itinerary

Below is the standard 7-day operating itinerary used for private departures. Flight times may vary slightly depending on weather, landing permissions, payload, and final route planning.

Day 1

Ulaanbaatar → Gobi Desert

Helicopter: 2.5–3 hours 4x4 drive: about 3 hours Stay: Luxury Gobi camp
  • Morning private helicopter departure from Ulaanbaatar to Dalanzadgad or a suitable Gobi landing zone.
  • Arrival assistance and transfer into private Land Cruisers. VIP setup is based on two guests per vehicle.
  • Drive to the Khongor Sand Dunes region for afternoon desert exploration.
  • Activities include camel riding, panoramic dune viewpoints, sunset photography, and chef-served dinner.
  • Overnight at a luxury Gobi camp such as Three Camel Lodge or equivalent standard.
Day 2

Full Gobi Exploration

4x4 drive: 5–6 hours total Meals: Breakfast, lunch, dinner Stay: Luxury Gobi camp
  • Private breakfast service followed by departure to Bayanzag, also known as the Flaming Cliffs.
  • Explore the famous fossil-bearing red cliffs and scenic desert formations.
  • Continue to Yol Valley for a canyon walk and seasonal ice gorge viewing.
  • Optional visit with a nomadic family depending on timing and local access.
  • Return to camp for dinner and overnight in the Gobi.
Day 3

Gobi → Bayan-Ulgii / Altai Region

Helicopter: 2–3 hours Local transfer: 1–2 hours Stay: Premium ger camp
  • Morning helicopter departure from the Gobi to western Mongolia.
  • Arrival in the Bayan-Ulgii region with private overland transfer to the evening camp location.
  • Cultural experience featuring Kazakh traditions and an eagle hunter presentation where available.
  • Full-board dining service and overnight at a premium ger camp or expedition lodge standard.
Day 4

Altai Tavan Bogd National Park

4x4 drive: 5–6 hours off-road Activities: Glacier views, trekking, horseback option Stay: Mountain expedition camp
  • Overland departure by private Land Cruisers deeper into the Altai Tavan Bogd area.
  • Scenic stops for mountain panoramas, valley viewpoints, and glacier-facing photo points.
  • Activity options include light trekking, horseback riding, and wildlife observation opportunities.
  • Private cook and assistant team support the expedition section with freshly prepared meals.
  • Overnight at a high-quality mountain expedition camp.
Day 5

Altai → Taiga Reindeer Region

Helicopter: 2–3 hours 4x4 transfer: 1–2 hours Horse access: 1–2 hours
  • Morning helicopter transfer toward the Tsagaannuur / Taiga access zone.
  • Arrival and transition to 4x4 transport, then horseback or local trail access where needed.
  • Visit the Tsaatan reindeer herder community and experience the forest-taiga landscape.
  • Traditional-style dinner and overnight in a Taiga camp setting.
Day 6

Taiga → Khuvsgul Lake

Helicopter: 1–2 hours Stay: Luxury lakeside lodge Meals: Full board
  • Morning departure from the Taiga region by private helicopter to Khuvsgul Lake.
  • Check-in at a lakeside luxury lodge or premium camp.
  • Afternoon relaxation and activities such as boating, horseback riding, shoreline walks, or private lakeside dining.
  • Final remote-wilderness overnight beside one of Mongolia’s most iconic landscapes.
Day 7

Khuvsgul Lake → Ulaanbaatar

Helicopter: 3–4 hours Departure day End of services
  • Breakfast at the lodge followed by private helicopter departure back to Ulaanbaatar.
  • Arrival transfer assistance and end of the expedition.
  • Optional post-tour city hotel, vehicle, or airport transfer can be arranged on request.
